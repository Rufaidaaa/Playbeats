About PlayBeats

PlayBeats is the world’s leading social sound platform where anyone can listen musics and share them everywhere. Listening, uploading sounds to PlayBeats lets people easily share them privately with their friends or publicly to blogs, sites and social networks. It takes just a click to share sounds to Twitter, Google+, Facebook and many more. PlayBeats can also be accessed from anywhere using the official Android apps, as well as hundreds of creation and sharing apps built on the PlayBeats platform.
